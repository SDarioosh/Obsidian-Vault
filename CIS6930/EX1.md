- Definition of Real-Time

	- allows end-users and creators to interact with the game, simulation, VR experience, etc. smoothly enough without and hitching or delay.

- Definition of Unity

	- Unity is a real-time game engine that now has tools for many creative endeavors

- What can you do in Unity?

	- you can create 3D games, 2D games, AR/VR experiences, simulations, animated cutscenes or movies, etc.

- Who are Real-Time Creators?

	- Unity creators who utilize the real-time 3D nature of unity to bring their ideas to life. This isn't just games but as discussed in the last question includes things even as far as car customization system for users to pick a car they want.
